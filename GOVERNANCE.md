\# Governance



This repository defines inspection and observability rules for governed systems.



\## Claims control

\- No compliance guarantees

\- No security guarantees

\- Inspection only applies to declared scope



\## Change control

\- Rules must be explicit

\- Additions must not introduce ambiguity

\- Removal preferred over expansion



\## Elevation readiness

Eligible for WFSL verification only when:

\- Licence is Apache 2.0

\- Inspection rules are deterministic

\- Verification evidence is committed
